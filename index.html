Anirban Samanta

I'm currently pursuing my PhD at the University of California - Davis, USA under the advisement of Dr. S. J. Ben Yoo.

I received my BEng. degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology - Mesra, India in 2012. I then moved to the US to continue my education at the University of California - Irvine, graduating with my MS in 2014. After spending some time in research and consulting roles, I returned to graduate school for my PhD in 2017, where I am currently working at the Next Generation Networking Systems laboratory.

Research Interests

My current research interest is in heterogeneous and monolithic integration of electronic and photonic integrated circuits, with particular focus in high-performance computing and data center applications. My expertise extends across the entire hardware prototype system development with experience in RF/optics/heat simulations with COMSOL, mixed-signal IC design (photonic-electronic circuits) with Cadence-Lumerical-IPKISS-Python toolchain with IC package design, PCB design with Allegro and FPGA firmware development with experience in both VHDL and Verilog.
